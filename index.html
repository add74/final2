Course Goals


1. Consider technology’s role in acts of making

I truly did not comprehend the ways in which technonolgy is able to optimize all careers. Technology allows for a whole new relam of buisness to be opened. Through my short period of time in this course I have discovered the multitude of ways that technology can improve practices. I work for a nonprofit and we often use tools like canva to create flyers. Having th skills to use indesign or illustrator could prove ver helpful for future fundraisers and events.

2. Leverage Design Principles.

In our reading weve gone over a plethora of design principles. I had a very minimal understanding of UX Design. I learned the ux is much more complex than just simply design for multiple mediums. One of the points in the reading that piqued my interest was the idea that you will need to convince others that your design is the right design. Design is so objective that there is no right or wrong answer and in ux you must market yourself and your design. Lesson 10 sort of talks about the rules of UX. Its all about building an expertise and perfecting your craft. I also enjoyed learning that psychology plays a big role in designing ux (since I am a psych major). Design is a game to convince the consumer/client that what's on display is something they need or love. Appealing with pathos will create the most successful content.

3.Recognize and respond to ethical concerns.

Weve addressed the fact that design can be stolen/ duplicated very easily. It is very simple to use the premade template designs on canva, but it feels unethical to change the color and pawn it off as your own design. Ethical design is complicated becuase so far most of the projects we have done in class duplicate other already made designs. If this were professional independent work i would struggle to cultivate design ideas on my own. I think inspiration plays a huge role in what designs get created.

4. Engage with others in a workshop environment

Workshops with fellow students have been extremely helpful. It is nice to soundboard ideas, hear their succeses, and their failures. Most times the issues i was running into were very relatable. I have been able to learn from my fellow classmates. Some of them are extremely tech savvy which has allowed me to learn new ways to do things through them. The beauty of technology is that there are a million ways to get from point A to point B.

5. Practice revision within and across media forms

Revisions are crucial to creating the perfect design. I used to assume i could make something once and be done with it, but when it comes to such intricate details revision is required.

6. Learn to present your work

Our UX design was my first time creating a study my work. I was surprised about how insightful my participants were about my design. It was fun to create logos and then edit them- I actually liked the edited versions better than the original. My only limitation was the part of the assignment that required us to use zoom. Some of my participants didn’t have zoom prior so it was a bit of an inconvenience to download it. Then my iPad wouldn’t allow me to record the sessions so I had to restart them on my laptop. Once I hit record on my laptop and finished a whole session I wasn’t able to access that recording it disappeared into the stratosphere.I attempted to download some apps that transcribe audio recording s but they all required money. So I ended up having my participants give written responses along with me recording what they said. I wish I was able to figure out the zoom aspect, but I think it was more logical and convenient for me to get my participants written responses. Other than that hiccup everything else went well. Creating the PowerPoint was smooth and I like how I designed it.
